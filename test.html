Welcome to ACME Comic & Books!

Peoria’s go-to for comics and collectibles! Explore our curated selection of classics, new releases, and rare finds. Whether you're a longtime fan or just starting out, we’re here to help you discover your next adventure!

Where Heroes and Stories Come to Life!

Acme Comics & Books, in Peoria, IL, is the area's leading comic book store serving Peoria, Tazewell, Knox, McClean, Woodford, Logan and surrounding counties since 1991. We carry comics, used action figures and toys, vintage vinyl records, DVDs, games, posters and CDs, graphic novels and much more. For your next comic book purchase, visit Acme Comics & Books in Peoria.
